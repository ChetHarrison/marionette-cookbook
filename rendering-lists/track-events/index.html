Simple Lists

These collection of lists are collectively called the simple lists. What makes them simple is that they're being rendered by an Item View, and not a Collection or Composite View. It might seem strange to render a list with an Item View, but there are times when you don't need the added functionality that those views give you.

When To Use

You should use Item Views to render your lists when you don't need to track events, like hovers or clicks, on individual items in the list. If you do want to do that then you should look at the other examples.
